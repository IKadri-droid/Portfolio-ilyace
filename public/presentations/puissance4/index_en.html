📖 THE STORY

🏰 The Context

In the distant lands of Pixel Kingdom, two rival lords battle for control: the Red Knight and the Yellow Paladin.

🔴 Red Knight

Bold and strategic
Master of offense

🟡 Yellow Paladin

Wise and calculating
Defense expert

⚔️ Whoever aligns 4 banners (TOKENS) in the Great Grid of Destiny becomes the legitimate sovereign!

🎲 THE RULES

⚡ Principle

  • 2 players compete
  • 6×7 Grid
  • Red starts

🎯 Objective

  • Successfully align 4 tokens in any direction: horizontal, vertical or diagonal.

🎮 Gameplay

1. Click column → 2. Token falls → 3. Turn ends → 4. Repeat

🏆 4 aligned tokens = VICTORY!
